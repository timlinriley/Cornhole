Instructions

  • Every round each player must throw all 4 of their bags to complete the round
  • If a bag lands and rests anywhere on the board, 1 point is added to the player's round score
  • If a bag lands in the hole 3 points are added to the players round score
  • After each round the total score is calculated using the cancellation method. The player with the most points in the round is awarded to their total score: their round score - other players round score
  • Gameplay continues until a player reaches 21 points in their total score
